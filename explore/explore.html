Nightjar

A team of scientists from the Swedish Museum of Natural History and elsewhere has described a new species of nightjar living in the tropical forests of Timor and Wetar, Lesser Sunda Islands. Nightjars are nocturnal birds that eat insects and are found all over the world, except in Antarctica and some islands.
